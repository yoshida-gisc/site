Welcome!

Takahiro Yoshida (吉田 崇紘) is Project Assistant Professor of Department of Urban Engineering, School of Engineering, The University of Tokyo, Japan. He received his Ph.D. degree in Policy and Planning Sciences from University of Tsukuba, 2018. His research interests include: geographical information sciences (GIS or GISc), remote sensing, spatial econometrics, spatial statistics, compositional data analysis, and urban big data analysis.
